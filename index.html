SecondHandHounds.org

Secondhandhounds is a non-profit foster based pet adoption agency based in Minnetonka MN. There mission is to provide safe shelter, proper veterinary care and daily necessities for animals at risk, while working hard to find each a permanent, loving home. Their website is in need of a style overhaul for both the mobile and desktop.

The Project

Worked with users to identify needs in a pet adoption agency website. Created favorites and filterable results when searching for dogs. Also created style guide that can be applied throughout the website for a consistent clean design.

My Role

Contributed to all stages of this project including user interviews, synthesizing the data into the affinity map, building and refining the prototypes in Figma for multiple versions of the mid and high fidelity for the Desktop and Mobile. Utilized user feedback to iterate along the way.

Insights

  • SHH is foster based, and needs to continue to focus on fundraising, fostering and events. We made sure to keep these elements easily accessible.
  • About half the users surveyed regularly look at pet adoption sites to brighten their day with pictures of cute pets. Prioritizing pictures of the animals.
  • A/B testing proved valuable to determine best placement of the heart feature. Additional testing can/should be done.

User Research

Conducted virtual interviews and sent out surveys to understand users needs of a pet adoption website. Used affinity map to gather feedback and identify trends in the results.

User Interview Feedback

Next Steps

This project was a great opportunity to update style on an existing site. We identified several other features that could be built out to further expand this site. These items were all called out by in our user interview process.

  • Build Health Card feature.
  • Ability to share pets on social media.
  • Build out Fostering Services of the site. Way to schedule viewing, and contact foster families.
  • Visual representation of next steps in the adoption process.
